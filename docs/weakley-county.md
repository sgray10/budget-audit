# Weakley County Notes

## Initial target packet

- Finance, Ways, and Means Committee meeting packet
- Meeting date: 2026-06-30
- Location listed in packet: Weakley County Board of Education conference room

The agenda includes budget amendments for FY ending 2026-06-30 and approvals for FY 2026-27 budgets across multiple funds, including General Fund, Solid Waste, Drug Control, Highway, General Purpose School, School Nutrition, Debt Service, General Capital Projects, Community Development, and Nursing Home.

## Initial questions

### Budget process

- Which amendments are true year-end cleanup versus material policy changes?
- Which changes are recurring versus one-time?
- Are budget amendments grouped in a way that obscures material line-item changes?
- Are proposed budgets comparable to prior actuals and current-year actuals?

### Compensation visibility

- Which salary-related lines are individually attributable?
- Which lines aggregate multiple positions or departments?
- Are benefits, insurance, retirement, and payroll taxes available separately?
- Can salary data be joined to position titles and departments without guessing?

### School funding

- Which pressures are structural: insurance, special education, staffing, state/federal formula changes?
- Which pressures are temporary: textbook adoption, vehicle replacement, unusual contract costs?
- Are state/federal funding changes tracked clearly enough to explain local-tax impact?

### Investment policy

- What changed between the original and amended investment policy?
- Who has delegated authority to invest idle county funds?
- What reports are required, how often, and are they public?
- Are investment reports reconciled against policy requirements?

Answered (with two flagged as needing a public-records follow-up) in `docs/checkpoints/weakley-fwm-2026-06-30-investment-policy-resolution-2026-52.md`, covering Resolution 2026-52 and its two attached policy exhibits.

## Suggested first-pass analyses

1. Fund-level budget comparison
   - Actual 24-25
   - Budget 25-26
   - Actual 25-26
   - Budget 26-27

2. Material delta report
   - Absolute dollar delta
   - Percent delta
   - Rank by largest changes
   - Flag new, eliminated, and materially changed line items

3. Salary and compensation index
   - Identify all line items containing salary, wages, overtime, benefits, insurance, retirement, social security, and related terms.
   - Classify as individual, departmental aggregate, or unclear.

4. Source transparency score
   - Can a citizen identify what the line means?
   - Can a citizen identify who or what receives the money?
   - Is the line tied to a department/fund/account code?
   - Is there a supporting explanation?

## Public posture

Keep the language neutral:

- `unclear`, not `hidden`
- `needs explanation`, not `suspicious`
- `does not reconcile in this extraction`, not `wrong`
- `aggregated line item`, not `concealed salary`

The goal is to produce better questions and cleaner public understanding.
